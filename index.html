How It Works

01
Leaf Classification
MobileNetV2 backbone fine-tuned on the Apple subset of PlantVillage dataset. Identifies 5 classes: Scab, Black Rot, Cedar Rust, Alternaria, Healthy.
02
Severity Segmentation
U-Net segmentation model separates diseased pixels from healthy tissue. Severity % = diseased pixels ÷ total leaf pixels × 100.
03
YOLO Fruit Counting
YOLOv8 trained on apple orchard images. Detects partially visible and overlapping fruits with bounding-box confidence scoring.
